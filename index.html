Anchor Institution Engagement Tool

This tool makes it simple for Penn students, staff, and neighbors to share quick, helpful feedback about places on and around campus. Click any spot on the map and complete a short form to say what you like, report a problem, or suggest an improvement. Submissions are routed to Penn Facilities and University City managers to guide practical improvements that make the campus better for everyone.

Before you continue

Please confirm you understand and agree to the following:

  • Your submission may be reviewed by Penn Facilities and University City District staff.
  • Do not include personal health information or sensitive data about others.
  • Feedback should describe a location on or near campus and suggested improvements.

How to use the form

  1. Pan and zoom to the place you want to comment on within the boundary.
  2. Click the map to drop a marker and open the quick feedback form.
  3. Describe the issue, compliment, or suggestion and submit your entry.

Tip: precise clicks and clear descriptions help staff respond faster.
